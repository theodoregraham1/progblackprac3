Computers in Medicine

In 2017, Mehta, Deb & Rao noted that "computers are being increasingly used in [the] medical profession". They described the importance of:

Statistical modelling
To quickly and accurately interpret statistical data
Laboratory computing
To provide accurate and rapid results to tests such as blood chemistry and then match these with patient identification details
Hospital information systems
To consistently transmit and store data and monitor this process

More recently, there have been advancements in using technologies such as computer vision or Convolutional Neural Networks to identify malignant and benign tumors.
